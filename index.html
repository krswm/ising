Ising Model

\[ \varepsilon_{x y} = \sum_{x' y'} J \sigma_{x y} \sigma_{x' y'} - H \sigma_{x y} \]


\(\sigma_{x y} = 1\): State A (Up spin / Filled lattice gas / Atom A on binary alloy)

\(\sigma_{x y} = - 1\): State B (Down spin / Absent lattice gas / Atom B on binary alloy)

If you’d like the spins to be half integers, you could just quarter \(J\) and halve \(H\).

Number of neighbors \(z\): 4 for 2D Ising model, 2 for 1D.

Magnetization (colors on the graph) \(\frac{M}{w h} = \frac{1}{w h} \sum \sigma_{x y}\)

Total energy (gray on the graph) \(\frac{U}{w h z} = \frac{1}{w h z} \sum \varepsilon_{x y}\)
